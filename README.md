## Bio

### Plan, Share, and Manage Events with Ease:
Whether you're organizing a scout trip, class project, or group event, our app makes it simple to create events, assign roles, track payments, and keep everyone in the loop.

<br>

### Create Events Fast:
Easily set up your event with a title, date, location, and custom description.

<br>

### Roster Management:
See who’s attending, assign roles, and toggle payment or attendance status—all in one place.

<br>

### Admin Features:
Admins can edit roles, promote others to admin, and manage the full roster.

<br>

### One-Tap Sharing:
Share your event with others instantly. Members can view details and join without confusion.

<br>

### Track Payments & Attendance:
Stay organized with built-in payment and attendance tracking.

<br>

### Simple, Secure, and Built for Groups:
Designed for teams, clubs, and any group that needs to stay on the same page.

<br>

### Built by a scout, made for scouts:
The entire purpose of the app is to streamline creating, planning, and managing scout events.
